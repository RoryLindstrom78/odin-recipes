Mee Ma's famous lasagna

Mee Ma's favorite lasagna, a recipe anybody can make and ANYBODY CAN ENJOY! Please note that Mee Ma is not responsible for any deaths as a result of her famous lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup love
  • 3 metric tons of battery acid
  • pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

Steps:

  1. Pour the sugar, love, and battery acid into a bowl and stir.
  2. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 45 minutes the pasta, with tomato sauce and cheese spread on it>
  3. pour the mixture you made in step 1 over the top and enjoy!
