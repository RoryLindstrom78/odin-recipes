Mee Ma's Brisket

A brisket to blow your head off! Just you wait until you see this, you won't believe it! It's finger lickin slurping delish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pure beef brisket
  • 2 liters barbecue sauce
  • 1 thermonuclear bomb

Directions:

  1. Cook the brisket in a smoker/on a gril for 4 hours
  2. Pour the barbecue sauce on afterwards
  3. Keep the thermonuclear bomb for yourself
  4. Enjoy!
